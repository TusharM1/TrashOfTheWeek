Overview

We are almost through month 8 of documenting the weekly trash. Usually, people wait until some big milestone to celebrate how consistent they have been, but every week I take these photos I remind myself of what it means to be consistent.

This week was a real average of what it’s all about. There’s nothing too out of the ordinary here, and maybe that’s a good thing. If every week is exciting, is any week really?

Three main categories this week: consumables, fragrance, and wrappers.

This week was almost a perfect snapshot of what the average American might consume in the course of a week. Everything from food and drink, to used containers and scraps, my trash had it all. One particular wrapper has an interesting story behind it… But first, let’s review this week’s trash!

Gallery

Consumables:

This category has everything to do with food and drink. The two things I consumed this week, an enchilada bowl and a cup of soda were very tasty but probably not that good for you. I did a bit of grocery shopping this week where I picked up a lot of food and drink, so I figured this would fit well into this category.

Fragrance:

This is a bit of a looser categorization, but I think it still fits pretty well. I got this AirWick a few weeks ago and it just finished. It was a fall season one anyway, no big deal. My body wash just finished but during my grocery shopping, I also picked up some more body wash since I was running out. Lastly, I just ran through the last of my hand sanitizer, I kind of liked clinical, toxic smell from the unscented kind.

Wrappers:

In this category, I combined everything that was a package containing something else. The mangled bag was from a shopping trip the week prior that I forgot to throw out. The polaroid packaging was from a friend who left it here when she opened it to take a polaroid photo. The keychain packaging was from something I received from a website called Chess.com, it was a very cool keychain in the shape of a chess pawn.

Item of the week: Keychain packaging.

This was my favorite item from the week because I received this as a gift from Chess.com, it is a really nice keychain, and it came in this cute little packaging. I put it on my keychain, and it really makes it stand out and identify it as my own.
